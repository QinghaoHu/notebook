Political parties are organizations that represent different political perspectives. 
Each political party has a political ideology - a specific set of beliefs and values. 

Socialism:
	want both economic and social equality and a big government to enforce it. 
liberalism
	What economic equality but social freedom. 
Conservatism:
	Want the government to protect social traditions but unhold economic freedom. 
Libertarianism:
	what social and economic freedom and a very small government.
